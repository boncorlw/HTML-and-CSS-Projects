2015 Harley-Davidson Street Bob

Overview

The 2015 Harley-Davidson Street Bob is part of the Dyna family, offering a stripped-down, classic bobber style with modern performance.

It features a Twin Cam 103 engine, a six-speed transmission, and a minimalist design that makes it a favorite among Harley enthusiasts.

Production Information

The 2015 Street Bob was produced between:

August 2014 – July 2015

More Production Details

The 2015 model year was manufactured at Harley-Davidson’s factory in York, Pennsylvania, and released as part of the **Dyna Glide** lineup.

Key features included:

  • 1690cc Twin Cam 103 engine
  • Blackout styling with mini-ape handlebars
  • Six-speed Cruise Drive transmission
  • Optional ABS and security system
